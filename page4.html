Oval Body Shape

Dressing Style for Oval/Apple Body Shape

Women's Clothing Recommendations

Choose:

  • A-line dresses that flow away from the body
  • Flared trousers or palazzos
  • Off-the-shoulder and strapless tops
  • Layering with light jackets or cardigans
  • Empire waistlines to define the bust

Pro Tip

Calf-length boots, strappy sandals, and wedges are perfect for balancing out your slim legs and highlighting your curves.

Men's Clothing Recommendations

Opt for:

  • Structured blazers that add definition to the shoulders
  • Fitted shirts to create a streamlined appearance
  • Dark, straight-leg trousers to elongate the legs
  • Avoid overly tight clothing; instead, go for tailored fits
  • Layering with lightweight outerwear to add depth
