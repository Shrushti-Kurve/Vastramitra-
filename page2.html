Triangle Body Shape

Dressing Style for Triangle Body Shape

Women's Clothing Recommendations

Choose:

  • Skinny jeans paired with loose tops
  • A-line skirts and wide-legged pants
  • Sweetheart or V-neck tops and dresses
  • Layered outfits with cardigans or structured blazers
  • Flutter sleeves and boat necklines

    • Pro Tip

    Choose open-toe wedges, and knee or thigh-high boots to distract attention from thighs and lengthen your legs.

Men's Clothing Recommendations

Choose:

  • Tailored jackets to broaden the shoulders
  • Structured shirts with patterns to add interest
  • Straight or relaxed-fit trousers
  • Layered looks with vests or lightweight jackets
  • Avoid overly tight clothing to maintain comfort
