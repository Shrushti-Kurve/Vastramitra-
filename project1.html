Characteristics:Equal width and length, soft curves, full cheeks.

Accessories: Earrings: Long, dangle earrings create the illusion of length.
Glasses: Angular frames (like square or rectangular) add definition.
Necklace Types: Opt for long, dangling necklaces or Y-shaped styles to create the illusion of length.

Characteristics:Strong jawline, wide forehead, equal width at the forehead and jaw.

Accessories: Earrings: Soft, rounded styles (like hoops or teardrops) help soften angular features.
Glasses: Round or oval frames balance the sharpness of the face.
Necklace Types: Rounded or curved necklaces, such as chokers or soft pendants, can soften angular features.

Characteristics:Narrow forehead and chin, wide cheekbones.

Accessories: Earrings: Oval or teardrop shapes to soften the cheekbones.
Glasses: Frames that are wider than the cheekbones, such as cat-eye or embellished styles.
Necklace Types:Opt for necklaces that sit on the collarbone or have rounded shapes, such as collars or soft pendants, to soften the cheekbones.

Characteristics:Wider forehead, narrow chin, cheekbones are prominent.

Accessories: Earrings: Chandelier or drop earrings that draw attention downwards.
Glasses: Oval frames that soften the forehead's width.
Necklace Types:Look for medium to long necklaces that draw attention downwards, like teardrop pendants.

Characteristics: Balanced proportions, slightly wider forehead, narrower chin.

Accessories: Earrings: Almost any style works, but hoops and drop earrings are particularly flattering.
Glasses: Rectangular or geometric frames enhance your features.
Necklace Types:Almost any style works! Try longer pendant necklaces or layered chains to complement the balanced proportions.

Characteristics: often referred to as a pear shape, features a narrow forehead, wider cheeks, and a broader jawline

Accessories: Earrings: Look for wider or statement earrings, such as chandelier or hoop styles, which can help balance the narrower forehead and draw attention upwards.
Glasses: Choose frames that are wider at the top, such as cat-eye or oval shapes, which can help balance the jawline and draw attention to the eyes.
Necklace Types: Long, flowing necklaces or V-shaped styles can help elongate the neck and draw attention upwards.
