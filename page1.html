Rectangle Body Shape

Dressing Style for Rectangle Body Shape

Women's Clothing Recommendations

Choose:

  • A-line skirts and dresses
  • Layered tops and ruffled designs
  • Sweetheart or V-necklines
  • Peplum tops to create curves
  • Structured blazers and long cardigans

    • Pro-Tip

    Simple pumps, textured shoes, and ballet flats will work best for a rectangle body shape, helping to create the illusion of elongated legs.

Men's Clothing Recommendations

Choose:

  • Tailored blazers to add structure
  • Layered looks with vests or cardigans
  • Shirts with patterns or textures to add dimension
  • Lightweight jackets to create interest
  • Dark, straight-leg trousers for a streamlined look
